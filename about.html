Our Story

FredElectrix was founded in 2023 by Fred, a certified electrical engineer with over 15 years of experience in the industry. What started as a small electrical repair service has grown into one of Kenya's leading electrical solutions providers.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality electrical services to homes and businesses across Kenya. Today, we've expanded to offer comprehensive solutions including solar installations, security systems, smart home automation, and a wide range of electrical products.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and sustainable electrical solutions that power progress and improve lives across Kenya. We are committed to delivering exceptional service while maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's leading electrical solutions provider, recognized for excellence in service delivery, innovation in sustainable energy solutions, and commitment to empowering communities through reliable electricity access. We envision a Kenya where every home and business has access to safe, efficient, and sustainable power.
