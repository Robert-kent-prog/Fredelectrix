Do Not Sell My Personal Information

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains your rights regarding the sale of your personal information and how to exercise them.

Important: FredElectrix does not sell your personal information to third parties. This page is provided to inform you of your rights under applicable data protection laws.

Your Data Protection Rights

Under the Data Protection Act, 2019 of Kenya and other applicable privacy laws, you have specific rights regarding your personal information. These include:

  • Right to Know: You can request information about the personal data we collect, use, disclose, and sell.
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information that we have collected.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can direct us not to sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
  • Right to Correct: You can request correction of inaccurate personal information.

Our Data Practices

Important Notice

FredElectrix does not sell your personal information to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. We only share information:

  • With service providers who assist us in delivering our services
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our customers and the public
  • In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition, etc.)

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise your data protection rights, you can:

Email Request

Send an email to privacy@fredelectrix.co.ke with your request details.

Phone Request

Call us at +254 720 123 456 during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM, Mon-Fri).

Verification Process

For security purposes, we may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests. This may require:

  • Providing information to match with our records
  • Responding to a verification email or phone call
  • Providing proof of identity for sensitive requests

Opt-Out Request Form

Use this form to submit a formal "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" request. Please note that while we don't sell your information, we will honor your request to ensure no future changes to our practices affect your preferences.

Response Timeline

We will respond to your request within:

  • 10 business days for acknowledgment of receipt
  • 45 days for substantive response (may be extended by an additional 45 days with notification)

If we cannot verify your identity or the request, we may deny the request and explain why.

Need Help?

If you have questions about your privacy rights or need assistance with your request, please contact our Privacy Team at privacy@fredelectrix.co.ke or call +254 720 123 456.
